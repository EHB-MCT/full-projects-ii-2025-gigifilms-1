Ana + Yek

Ana + Yek (Me + You) follows twin sisters Sanaa and Zohra. What begins as Zohra's exploration of their Moroccan heritage gradually reveals the shifting dynamics of their relationship, as Sanaa's deepening love for Islam becomes more apparent. The film unfolds as a journey of mutual understanding — a cinematic dialogue fueled by their desire to connect. An intimate portrait of sisterly love and independence.

Behind GIGI films

Zohra Benhammou & Romy Mana are a filmmakers duo based in Brussels. Their creative partnership began by chance and blossomed into their debut feature documentary, Ana + Yek (Me + You).
As writers and directors, they work beyond genres and formats, exploring (auto)biography, relational dynamics, spaces and places. They seek honest, direct images, always prioritizing relevance and sincerity in their playful, intuitive approach.
